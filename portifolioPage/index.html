What Is Data Engineering

Data engineering is the process of designing, constructing, and maintaining data systems that efficiently store, process, and analyze large datasets. It involves building data pipelines to collect, transform, and deliver data to various stakeholders for decision-making and analysis. Data engineers work closely with data scientists, analysts, and other technical professionals to ensure that data is accessible, reliable, and secure. They utilize various tools and technologies, such as databases, data warehouses, data lakes, and cloud platforms, to create scalable and efficient data infrastructure.
